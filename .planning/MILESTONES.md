**Delivered:** A fully interactive 3D solar system orrery with cinematic camera fly-to, responsive UI, and AU Brussel branding — deployable as a static portfolio site.

**Phases completed:** 1-4 (8 plans total)

**Key accomplishments:**

- Cinematic GSAP camera fly-to system with synchronized OrbitControls and keyboard shortcuts
- Interactive discovery: hover labels, orbit glow, InfoPanel with stats, fun facts carousel, composition charts, notable moons
- Complete UI overlay: NavSidebar, TimelineControl (0-100x speed), AU Brussel footer badge
- Responsive mobile design: hamburger drawer, bottom sheet InfoPanel, full touch isolation
